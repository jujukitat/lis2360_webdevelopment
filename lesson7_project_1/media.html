Media

Sites to listen to music on

YouTube YouTube is one of the most common webistes to listen to music on. Users can look up a variety of videos, such as lyric videos, music videos, or just simply music.
Spotify While Spotify is free, you can buy a monthly subscription to get rid of the ads and be able to have more freedom with your listening.
Pandora Pandora is a lot similar to Spotify except it plays stations based on music songs you previously listened to. There are a variety of playlists you can choose from.
iTunes Anyone who has an Apple iPhone, iPad, or iPod can use iTunes. It is where you can buy songs, albums, and even music videos to listen to on your device.
